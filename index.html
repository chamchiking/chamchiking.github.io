Mincheol Cha

I am a Master’s student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University, advised by Professors Hyuk-Jae Lee and Xuan Truong Nguyen.

My research focuses on Domain-Specific Accelerators (DSAs), high-level synthesis (HLS), reconfigurable computing with FPGAs, and neuromorphic computing. I am passionate about designing scalable and energy-efficient architectures to address real-time, high-performance computational challenges.

I received my B.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Seoul National University, graduating cum laude, and am currently pursuing my M.Sc. with a focus on reconfigurable hardware and sensor integration for real-time systems.
